Arriving by Car

The Hub is situated adjacent to Edinburgh Castle in the very heart of Edinburgh's historic Old Town. It is less than half a mile from Waverly train station and 7 miles from Edinburgh International Airport.

Arriving from Airport

Paid on street and disabled parking is available on johnson Terrace and there is an NCP car park located on Castle Terrace which is a short walk from The Hub
