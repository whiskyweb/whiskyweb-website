Whisky Web II

April 12-13 2013

A web conference created for the web community, by the web community, Whisky Web will have something to offer everyone who works with the web, be they a designer, a developer or something inbetween. Get your geek on in Scotland's inspiring capital.

We couldn't have hoped for a better innaugural event and we're raising the bar even higher for next April. If you're interested in a mix of conventional conference talks and fun and entertaining activities to help you get to know the other delegates, then keep April 12th - April 13th free in your calendar! Watch this space for more information.

Tell Me More!

This time around, we'll be taking our delegates on a litle bit of an excursion. The venue for the last conference was great, but we know we can do better. And in 2013 we'll be hosting a residential conference experience at the stunning Airth Castle, nestled in the beautiful surroundings of Stirlingshire. We'll be packing in plenty of talks from top-notch speakers and some fun activities on the first day, followed by an after-party, then returning to Edinburgh for a second day of hacking and socialising.

What the shark!?

You may have noticed a few subtle references of a shark nature dotted around the site. Those of you who were at the last conference may get the reference! For the benefit of those who don't, we had a "...frickin' shark with laser beams..." &endash; well not quite, but we certainly had a couple of radio-controlled airbourne sharks roaming the main hall! We certainly hope they'll be kind enough to grace us with their presence this time around!
