WHISKY WEB II
APR 12-13, 2013
Whisky Web 2012 About The Event Follow On Twitter

At the start of this year, a small group of developers were inspired to take it upon themselves to organise the first Whisky Web conference. With experience attending conferences but certainly not organising them, they worked tirelessly to a tight schedule to host, by all accounts a successful, unique and entertaining event for all attendees.

Well that was this year, and work is already underway to organise a bigger and better event for 2013. It will feature a castle in the picturesque heart of Scotland; there will almost certainly be inflatable sharks to oversee things; a heady mix of inspirational first-class speakers and activities to keep the mind and body energised and much, much more.

A full site will be coming in January, with more detailed information about all aspects of the conference, including prices. To keep up with the latest information from the team, follow @WhiskyWeb on twitter or drop in on the #WhiskyWeb IRC channel on Freenode. Alternatively, you can drop us an email at info@whiskyweb.co.uk if you have any specific questions, or if you want to talk to us about sponsorship opportunities. From the entire team, we look forward to seeing you next year!
